Agebitashi

Description

Agebitashi refers to deep fried vegetables which are soaked in a dashi-soy based marinade.

This recipe is only made with eggplant, however, capsicum, onion, zucchini and beans can also be used.

Ingredients

  • oil to deep fry
  • two eggplants
  • 300ml dashi stock
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 300ml hot water

Steps

  1. Combine the dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin and hot water in flat container.
  2. Cut eggplant into 5-10cm pieces.
  3. Deep fry the eggplant in pan for about 1 minute , flipping halfway through.
  4. Transfer the eggplant onto rack to drip off excess oil.
  5. Place the eggplant into the dashi marinade prepared in step 1.
  6. Keep in fridge up to 3 days.
